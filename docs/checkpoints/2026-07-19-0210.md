   it lower down).
5. On Unix, `reserve_aligned_lazy_raw` already does a full eager `mmap` (the
   whole segment is immediately usable), but the allocator's OWN frontier
   bookkeeping still tracks a fake "lazy" partial frontier and does periodic
   logical grow-on-carve bookkeeping (bounds checks, `commit_range` no-op
   calls, atomic counter bumps) for ZERO benefit — pure overhead. This is a
