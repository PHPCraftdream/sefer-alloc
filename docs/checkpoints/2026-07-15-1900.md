The implementing agent identified and closed a genuine "wedge hazard" (a Vyukov-ring's irreversible tail-CAS reservation composing badly with a fallible lazy-allocation step, which could permanently wedge the drain side on OOM) by ensuring the sidecar exists BEFORE attempting the tail CAS, not after. Combined round-1+round-2 reduction from the original baseline: ~129.5 MiB → ~4.52 MiB (~28.6x).
